[SECTION: ABOUT]
The DST-Centre for Interdisciplinary Mathematical Sciences (DST-CIMS) is an 
academic and research centre at Banaras Hindu University dedicated to 
interdisciplinary research and education in Mathematical Sciences, Statistics, 
Computing, Artificial Intelligence, Data Science, Scientific Computing, 
Mathematical Modelling and related fields.

The centre was formed through the collaborative initiative of the Departments 
of Mathematics, Statistics, Computer Science, and Applied Mathematics at BHU. 
Over time, researchers from several other departments have also contributed 
to and participated in its activities.

The centre promotes innovation, collaboration, academic excellence, and 
advanced research through interdisciplinary approaches that connect 
mathematics, statistics, computer science, and emerging technologies. 
DST-CIMS serves students, researchers, faculty members, and the broader 
academic community through research activities, academic programmes, 
workshops, seminars, conferences, and collaborative projects.
