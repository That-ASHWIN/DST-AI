DST CENTRE FOR INTERDISCIPLINARY MATHEMATICAL SCIENCES (DST-CIMS), BANARAS HINDU UNIVERSITY (BHU)
Official department knowledge base.

[SECTION: ABOUT]
CIMS BHU stands for the DST Centre for Interdisciplinary Mathematical Sciences at Banaras Hindu University. CIMS BHU is located at Banaras Hindu University (BHU), Varanasi - 221005, Uttar Pradesh, India. CIMS BHU operates under the Institute of Science at Banaras Hindu University. CIMS BHU was established with funding from the Department of Science and Technology (DST), Government of India. CIMS BHU was established vide letter number SR/S4/MS:428/07 dated 20 November 2007. The primary mission of CIMS BHU is to improve the overall quality of research and education in the Mathematical Sciences in Uttar Pradesh and adjoining areas. CIMS BHU aims to bring needed expertise and facilities to one convenient location for Mathematical Sciences research. CIMS BHU imparts training and research in Mathematical Sciences. CIMS BHU establishes core-group research facilities in the Mathematical Sciences region.

[SECTION: OBJECTIVES]
The objective of CIMS BHU is to improve the quality of research and education in Mathematical Sciences in Uttar Pradesh and adjoining areas. [SECTION: PROGRAMMES AND COURSES]
CIMS BHU offers three postgraduate (M.Sc.) programmes, all of which are 2 years (4 semesters) in duration. The programmes managed by DST-CIMS are:
1. M.Sc. in Statistics and Computing - Postgraduate programme, duration 2 years. Eligibility: as per Banaras Hindu University rules.
2. M.Sc. in Computational Science and Applications - Postgraduate programme, duration 2 years. Eligibility: B.Sc. in Computer Science / BCA / B.Tech in Computer Science / B.Tech in IT and equivalent subjects.
3. M.Sc. in Mathematics and Computing - Postgraduate programme, duration 2 years. Eligibility: as per Banaras Hindu University rules.
The full programme list, syllabi (Download links) and eligibility details are available on the official DST-CIMS Programme page: https://www.bhu.ac.in/site/Programme/1_233_3537_DST-Centre-for-Interdisciplinary-Mathematical-Sciences-Programme
CIMS BHU is also involved in Ph.D. doctoral research programmes across Mathematics, Statistics, Computer Science, and Applied Mathematics.

[SECTION: ADMISSION AND ELIGIBILITY]
Admission to the M.Sc. programmes at CIMS BHU is generally done through the CUET-PG national-level entrance examination conducted for postgraduate admissions at Banaras Hindu University. Ph.D. admissions at CIMS BHU follow the standard Banaras Hindu University research admission process, such as RET or applicable national fellowships. Eligibility for CIMS BHU programmes typically requires a relevant bachelor's or master's degree with the percentage specified by Banaras Hindu University. Please contact the CIMS office for the exact current intake numbers, eligibility cut-offs, and admission calendar, or check the BHU admission portal at https://admission.bhu.ac.in.

[SECTION: BHU ADMISSION PORTAL (UG AND PG)]
All admissions to Banaras Hindu University (BHU), including programmes offered through DST-CIMS, are handled centrally through the official BHU Admission Portal at https://admission.bhu.ac.in. The admission brochure is called the Information Bulletin and the latest UG and PG Information Bulletins (admission brochures) can be downloaded from the BHU admission portal at https://admission.bhu.ac.in.
Undergraduate (UG) admission at BHU: UG admission is based on the Common University Entrance Test for Undergraduate programmes, CUET-UG, conducted by the National Testing Agency (NTA). Candidates must have passed (or be appearing in) the 10+2 / Senior Secondary examination from a recognised board. Candidates register and apply online through the BHU CUET-UG portal at https://bhucuet.samarth.edu.in. The detailed UG eligibility, programmes, seats and procedure are given in the UG Information Bulletin available on https://admission.bhu.ac.in. BHU offers a wide range of UG programmes such as B.A., B.A. (Hons.), B.Sc., B.Sc. (Hons.), B.Com., B.Ed., B.F.A., B.P.A., LL.B., B.A. LL.B., B.Pharm., MBBS and many more.
Postgraduate (PG) admission at BHU: PG admission is based on the Common University Entrance Test for Postgraduate programmes, CUET-PG, conducted by the National Testing Agency (NTA). Candidates must hold a relevant bachelor's degree with the minimum percentage specified by BHU. Candidates register and apply online through the BHU CUET-PG portal at https://bhucuetpg.samarth.edu.in. After results, allotment is done through the Combined Allotment Program (CAP-PG). The detailed PG eligibility, programmes, seats and procedure are given in the PG Information Bulletin available on https://admission.bhu.ac.in. BHU offers PG programmes such as M.A., M.Sc., M.Com., M.Ed., and the M.Sc. programmes offered by DST-CIMS.
CUET-PG registration fee (indicative, verify on the portal): about Rs. 300 for UR / OBC-NCL / EWS candidates and about Rs. 150 for SC / ST / PwBD candidates, per programme. PhD admission at BHU is done through the Research Entrance Test (RET) or applicable national fellowships. For admission help, the official BHU admission helpdesk email is admission.help@bhu.ac.in. Admission dates, eligibility and bulletins change every year, so candidates should always verify the latest details on https://admission.bhu.ac.in before applying.
